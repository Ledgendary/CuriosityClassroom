Welcome to The Curiosity Classroom

At The Curiosity Classroom, we believe that learning should be an exciting adventure! Located in Churchdown, our tutoring service is the heartfelt creation of Kirsty Haines, an experienced educator who understands that every child learns uniquely. We specialise in creating personalised, hands-on learning experiences for primary school children that spark joy and build confidence while addressing individual academic needs.

What sets us apart is our genuine connection with each child. We don't just teach curriculum, we nurture curiosity, foster problem-solving skills, and cultivate a growth mindset in a supportive environment. Your child isn't just another student at The Curiosity Classroom; they're a young explorer embarking on an educational journey tailored specifically for them.

Our approach transforms traditional tutoring into something children actually look forward to attending. Parents consistently tell us how their once-reluctant learners now eagerly anticipate their sessions, showing improved confidence both academically and personally. If you're looking for more than just academic support, if you want your child to discover the genuine excitement of learning, we invite you to join our community of curious young minds!

Kirsty Haines, Founder


LEARNING PRINCIPLES

Every child is unique. We tailor our teaching approaches to match individual learning styles, strengths, and needs, ensuring your child receives the specific support they require to excel academically while developing a love of learning.

We deliver curriculum mastery through methods that captivate children's imagination and interest. By making learning relevant and exciting, we help students not only meet but exceed academic expectations, building strong foundations for future educational success.

Our supportive environment encourages children to ask questions, tackle challenges, and celebrate their progress. This nurtures resilience and self-belief that translates into improved performance in school and a positive approach to learning that serves them throughout life.
